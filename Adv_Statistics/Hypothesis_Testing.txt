Q. What is Hypothesis?

A. An idea or explanation of something that is based on a few known facts but that has not yet been proved to be a true or correct

   Testing a claim that is supposed to change the current facts
   
   
hypothesis ==> idea + with some facts

================================================================================================================================================
Null Vs Alternate Hypothesis:::
-------------------------------

Null Hypothesis (H0)         -->     There is no change from the stated facts

Alternate  Hyp. (Ha)         -->     The stated facts are correct
